## üåü Key Features

### 1. Smart Dashboard & Analytics
**Gain actionable insights into your network and performance.**

*   **Real-Time Metrics**: Instantly view total connections, lead quality distribution, and campaign performance.
*   **Industry Breakdown**: Visual charts showing exactly which industries your network covers, with drill-down capabilities into sub-industries.
*   **Lead Quality Scoring**: Automatically categorizes leads into **Primary** (High Value), **Secondary** (Potential), and **Tertiary** (Low Priority) based on your ideal customer profile.
*   **Time-Based Filtering**: Analyze data by Daily, Weekly, Monthly, or Yearly views to track growth trends.
*   **Export Ready**: Seamlessly import/export data via CSV or Excel for external reporting.

### 2. Intelligent Campaign Management
**Run targeted outreach campaigns with precision.**

*   **Multi-Type Campaigns**: Support for various outreach goals including:
    *   **Standard Outreach**: Connection requests and follow-ups.
    *   **Event Promotion**: Drive attendance to webinars and events.
    *   **Nurture Sequences**: Long-term relationship building.
    *   **Re-engagement**: Waking up dormant leads.
*   **Goal Tracking**: Set specific objectives (e.g., "Book Meetings", "Grow Network", "Build Pipeline") and track progress against them.
*   **Lifecycle Management**: Easily Draft, Launch, Pause, and Resume campaigns.
*   **Performance Tracking**: Monitor response rates, leads processed, and overall campaign health in real-time.

### 3. Automated Lead Discovery
**Never run out of high-quality prospects.**

*   **Smart Network Extraction**: Automatically scans your existing network to identify hidden opportunities.
*   **Stealth Automation**: Uses advanced "human-like" browsing behavior (Phantom Controller) to keep your account safe while working 24/7.
*   **Contact Sync**: Automatically syncs and updates contact information from LinkedIn.
*   **Smart Filtering**: Filter leads by connection degree (1st, 2nd, 3rd), industry, and more to find the perfect match.

### 4. AI & Engine Intelligence
**Powered by advanced AI for better results.**

*   **AI Integration**: Built with state-of-the-art AI (Claude/OpenAI) to assist in drafting personalized messages and analyzing lead potential.
*   **Smart Approvals**: A dedicated review system allows you to manually approve or reject specific actions before they happen, giving you full control over the automation.
*   **Content Engine**: Tools to help generate and manage engaging content for your outreach.

### 5. Multi-Channel Outreach
**Reach your prospects where they are.**

*   **LinkedIn Automation**: Connection requests, messages, profile visits.
*   **Email Integration**: Seamlessly sends emails via integrated providers (SendGrid/AWS SES) to complement your LinkedIn efforts.
*   **Unified Inbox**: (Planned/In-Progress) Manage responses from multiple channels in one place.
